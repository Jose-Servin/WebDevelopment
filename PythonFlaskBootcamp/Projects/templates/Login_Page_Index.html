MATH 152 - Engineering Calculus II

Instructor: Baker Servin

Spring 2018

Welcome to Math 152! Here you can find helpful links and course information.

This course focuses on Engineering Mathematics II. Differentiation and integration techniques and their applications (area, volumes, work), improper integrals, approximate integration, analytic geometry, vectors, infinite series, power series, Taylor series, computer algebra.
